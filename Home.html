MRU Student Mental Health Resource Guide

What Can I Find on This Website?

This site is as a comprehensive guide to the many public and private resources and supports available for Mount Royal University community at large. Occasionally one may face difficulties that put their academic and personal success at risk. Students, staff, and faculty have access to the support both on and off campus when things get tough. With early recognition and access to our many resources, mental health/addiction concerns can be identified and treated. Strengthening our collective commitment to the community at large.

 

Mount Royal University’s Mission
 

We are committed to personalized, experiential and outcome-based learning. We deliver highquality programs built on a foundation of General Education. Recognizing that diversity strengthens us all, Mount Royal University welcomes, supports and celebrates all of it its communities. We are also dedicated to fostering an environment of respect and inclusion. Dynamic, relevant and responsive to our students and communities, we are making MRU is Canada’s premier undergraduate University.
